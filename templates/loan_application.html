Loan Policy

The following loan policy applies to all loans provided by Creditrust. Please read the policy carefully:

Interest Rates and Loan Brackets

  • Loans up to 500,000 UGX: Start at 15% interest rate:
  • Loans beyond 500,000 UGX: Start at 10% interest rate:
  • Loans between 50,000 and 300,000 UGX: Increase daily at the initial rate to reach 35% by the end of 8 weeks (56 days).
  • Loans starting from and above 300,000 UGX: Increase daily at the initial rateto reach 30% by the end of 12 weeks (84 days).
  • Loans exceeding 1,000,000 UGX: The maximum interest rate is capped at 25% for a 14 weeks (100 days) repayment period.

Loan Application Process

  • Fill out the loan application form with accurate and complete information.
  • Submit the form for review by our loan officers.
  • Wait for a confirmation email or call regarding the status of your application.
  • If approved, follow the instructions provided to receive your loan.

Repayment and Penalties

  • Repayment must be made within the agreed period to avoid penalties.
  • Failure to repay on time will result in a fine and the loan will keep increasily at a similar daily linear rate.
  • Unpaid loans beyond the final bracket will incur additional fines, and the repayment cycle will restart with respective interest rates.
  • Creditrust provides loans based on trust and expects you to fulfill your obligation by clearing your loan.

By applying for a loan, you acknowledge and agree to abide by this loan policy.
